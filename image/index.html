WebP is the best choice for websites — it is 25-35% smaller than JPG at equivalent quality and supports transparency like PNG. For maximum compatibility with older browsers and email clients, JPG (photos) and PNG (graphics) remain safe defaults.
HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's default photo format on iPhone and iPad since iOS 11. It is more efficient than JPG but not supported by most Windows software, non-Apple applications or web platforms. Converting to JPG ensures universal compatibility.
PNG to PNG, WebP lossless to PNG, and similar lossless conversions preserve full quality. JPG and lossy WebP always involve some quality loss, though at 92% quality the difference is visually imperceptible for most images.
