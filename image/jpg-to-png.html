When Should You Use PNG Instead of JPG?

PNG is better for graphics, logos, screenshots and images with text where compression artefacts would be visible. JPG compression can cause visible artefacts around sharp edges and text in non-photographic content.

PNG also supports transparency (alpha channel), making it the right choice for images that need to blend with different backgrounds on websites or presentations.
