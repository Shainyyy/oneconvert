Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. File Processing

All file processing on OneConvert happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files — PDFs, images and other documents — are never uploaded to any server. They never leave your device.

2. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised usage data (page views, device type, country). This data does not include any file contents or personal identifiers.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalised ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings.

4. Cookies

We use cookies only for analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google AdSense). No cookies are used to track file contents or personal data.

5. Contact

For privacy questions: hello@oneconvert.net
