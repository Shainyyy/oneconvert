Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Applies to: oneconvert.net

1. At a Glance

Your files never leave your device. All file processing on OneConvert (PDF operations, image conversions, QR generation) runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No file data is ever transmitted to any server.

We collect anonymised usage data via Google Analytics (with your consent) and display advertisements via Google AdSense to keep the service free.

2. Controller (Responsible Party)

The controller responsible for data processing on this website within the meaning of the GDPR is:

OneConvert
oneconvert.net
Contact: hello@oneconvert.net

3. Data We Collect

📄 File Data — Not Collected

Files you upload or process are handled exclusively in your browser memory (RAM). They are never sent to our servers. We have no technical ability to access your files.

📊 Usage Analytics (with consent)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised statistics: pages visited, country, device type, session duration. This data does not identify you personally. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time via the cookie settings below.

📢 Advertising (with consent)

We display advertisements via Google AdSense. With your consent, Google may use cookies to personalise ads based on your browsing history. Without consent, only non-personalised ads are shown. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).

🔧 Technical Data (automatically)

Standard web server and CDN logs may include your IP address, browser type and access timestamp. These are required for technical operation and security. Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Logs are retained for up to 30 days.

6. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15–21)

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — You can request information about what data we hold about you (Art. 15)
  • Right to rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
  • Right to erasure — You can request deletion of your data (Art. 17)
  • Right to restriction — You can request limited processing of your data (Art. 18)
  • Right to object — You can object to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21)
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You can complain to your national supervisory authority (in Switzerland: EDÖB ↗; in the EU: your local DPA)

To exercise your rights, contact: hello@oneconvert.net

7. Data Security

This website is served exclusively over HTTPS (TLS encryption). All file processing is local to your browser — no file data transits any network. The website is hosted on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare CDN, both of which maintain enterprise-grade security infrastructure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The current version is always available at oneconvert.net/privacy.html. The date at the top indicates when it was last updated.
