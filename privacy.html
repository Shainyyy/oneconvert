Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · oneconvert.net

1. Who we are

OneConvert is a free online converter tool available at oneconvert.net.
Contact for privacy matters: hello@oneconvert.net

2. Your files — never uploaded

All file processing on OneConvert (PDF operations, image conversions, QR code generation, unit conversions, text and colour tools) runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are read into browser memory and processed locally. No file data is ever sent to any server. We have no technical means to access your files.

3. What data we collect

Analytics (only with your consent)

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised statistics about how the site is used — pages visited, approximate country, device type. This data does not identify you. Analytics only activates after you accept cookies in the cookie banner.

Advertising (only with your consent)

We display ads via Google AdSense to fund the free service. After you accept cookies, Google may personalise ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings ↗. Without consent, only non-personalised ads are shown.

Hosting infrastructure

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages and delivered via Cloudflare CDN. These services may process standard web request data (such as IP addresses) as part of their infrastructure operation. This is outside our control — refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement ↗ and Cloudflare's Privacy Policy ↗.

5. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Since we collect minimal data and no file content, the main data subject to these rights is analytics data (which can be deleted through your Google account).

To withdraw cookie consent or exercise any data right, contact: hello@oneconvert.net

You may also lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your country. In Switzerland: EDÖB ↗. In the EU: your national DPA.

6. Minors

OneConvert is a general-purpose tool service not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect any data from persons under the age of 16. If you believe a minor has used this service and provided personal data, contact us at hello@oneconvert.net and we will address it promptly.

7. Changes

We may update this policy when necessary. The current version is always at oneconvert.net/privacy.html.
