When Do You Need to Reorder PDF Pages?

Reordering pages is useful after scanning a multipage document that came out in the wrong sequence, when combining sections from different sources, or when preparing a presentation where slide order needs to change. Instead of re-exporting from the original application, reorder directly in your browser in seconds.

Upload your PDF and you will see numbered page thumbnails. Drag them into your desired order, then click Save. The reordered PDF downloads immediately.
