When Do You Need to Add Page Numbers to a PDF?

PDFs created from presentations, exports or scanned documents often have no page numbers. Adding them makes long documents much easier to navigate — particularly useful for reports, contracts, academic papers and instruction manuals. Most PDF tools charge for this feature or require a subscription.

Choose your numbering format (simple numbers, "Page X", "Page X of Y"), position (bottom centre is most common), and starting number. Click Add Page Numbers and your numbered PDF downloads instantly.
