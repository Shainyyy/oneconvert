What Is a PDF Watermark Used For?

PDF watermarks are used to mark documents as confidential, draft versions, samples, or internal use only. They appear across the page content, making it clear that the document is not final or should not be distributed. Businesses use watermarks on contracts, legal documents, reports and presentation drafts.

OneConvert adds the watermark text directly to the PDF page structure using pdf-lib — entirely in your browser. No upload, no server processing. Your document stays private.
